**Pixel Panic** is a fast-paced, retro-style racing and arcade game where players dodge obstacles, upgrade their car, and race to the finish line. With both **3D endless mode** and **2D arcade mode**, Pixel Panic brings the nostalgia of pixel graphics with modern gameplay mechanics.  

![Pixel Panic Screenshot](Logo.png)

---

## 🚗 Features

- **Endless 3D Mode** – Drive through procedurally generated roads and avoid hazards.  
- **Mini 2D Arcade Mode** – Retro pixel racing fun.  
- **Custom Map Builder** – Design your own tracks and share them.  
- **Garage Upgrades** – Improve traction, armor, and nitro boosts.  
- **Pixel Art Graphics** – Classic pixel rendering in 3D and 2D modes.  
- **Secret Cheat Codes** – Unlock hidden abilities and power-ups.  
- **Dynamic Music & Sounds** – Music adapts to gameplay intensity.

---

## 🎮 Controls

| Key | Action |
|-----|--------|
| W | Accelerate |
| S | Brake / Reverse |
| A | Steer Left |
| D | Steer Right |
| SHIFT | Nitro Boost |
| F3 | Debug Menu |
